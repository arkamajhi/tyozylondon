Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our end of tenancy cleaning process.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies or equipment?
No. Our professional teams arrive fully equipped with all necessary commercial-grade chemicals, vacuums, mops, and cloths. You don't need to supply a thing.
Do I have to be present during the clean?
Not at all. You can let our team in and leave, or arrange for us to collect keys from a local estate agent or secure lockbox. We will lock up when finished.
Are appliance cleans (oven, fridge, washing machine) included?
Yes! A full deep clean of your oven, stovetop, extractor fan, fridge/freezer (must be defrosted beforehand), and washing machine are strictly included in our standard end of tenancy checklist.
How does the 72-hour guarantee work?
If your inventory clerk or landlord finds any issues with the clean within 72 hours, simply send us the report. We will send a team back to address those specific areas at no extra cost to ensure you get your deposit back.
How many cleaners will arrive at my property?
Depending on the size of the property and the condition, we typically send a team of 2 to 4 professional cleaners. This ensures the job is done swiftly and to the highest standard without taking up your entire day.
Are there any hidden costs?
Absolutely not. The quote we provide based on the size and condition of your property is the final price. Parking fees, ULEZ, or congestion charges only apply if no free parking is available near the property and will be discussed upfront.
Is carpet and upholstery cleaning included?
Standard end of tenancy cleaning includes thorough vacuuming. Hot water extraction (steam cleaning) for carpets, sofas, or mattresses is an optional extra that can be added to your booking for a discounted rate.
What is your cancellation policy?
We understand that moving dates can change. We offer free cancellation or rescheduling up to 48 hours before your booking time.
